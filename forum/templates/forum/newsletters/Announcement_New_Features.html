Hi there! 👋

We shipped three helpful updates to the app and forum—profiles in mobile, polls, and volunteer hours tracking. Here’s how to use them.

Volunteer Hours Tracking

Presenting an easy way to view your volunteer hours!

Get started: Go open the volunteer hour tab on the side to view your hours

Profiles in the Mobile App

View and edit profiles from the mobile app: profile pictures, bios, and social media links are available without using the desktop site.

You can now also view other people's profiles within the mobile app.

Try it: Open the app, tap the menu, and select "Profile".

Polls — Faster Community Decisions

Create polls when posting or vote in threads to help the community decide on events, topics, and more.

Tip: Use polls for meetups, study groups, and feature requests.

If you want these enabled for a group or need help finding a setting, reply to this email or visit your account settings in the app.
